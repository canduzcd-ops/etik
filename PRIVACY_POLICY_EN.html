1. Introduction

ETIK ("Application") is an AI-powered ethical decision-making assistant developed by Raca Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Name/Nickname: Optional name or nickname you provide for personalized experience
  • Ethical Dilemmas: The dilemma texts you write for analysis
  • Personality Test Results: Your test responses for personalized mode
  • Preferences: Language, theme, and profile mode preferences

2.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Local Storage: All user data is stored only on your device using localStorage
  • Analysis History: Your saved analyses are stored locally on your device

2.3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI: The dilemma texts you enter for ethical analysis are sent to Google Gemini API. For Google's data usage policies: Google AI Terms

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze the ethical dilemmas you enter and provide philosophical perspectives
  • To provide a personalized analysis experience (if personalized mode is active)
  • To save your application preferences (theme, language)
  • To store your analysis history on your device

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. Local Storage

All your personal data (name, preferences, saved analyses) is stored only on your device. No user data is stored on Raca Labs servers.

4.2. API Communication

Dilemma texts sent for analysis are transmitted to Google Gemini API over HTTPS. This data is used only for analysis purposes and is not stored by Raca Labs.

4.3. Data Deletion

Since all your data is on your device:

  • All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can manually delete via Settings ‚Üí Reset Data
  • You can delete localStorage data by clearing browser cache

5. Data Sharing

Raca Labs does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Exceptions:

  • Google Gemini AI: Only the dilemma texts necessary for analysis
  • Legal Requirements: In case of legal processes, court orders, or government requests

6. Children's Privacy

Our Application is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
